Biju Patnaik

Biju Patnaik, in full Bijayananda Patnaik, (born 5 March 1916, Bellaguntha, Ganjam, Odisha—died 17 April 1997), Indian politician and aviator who played a leading role in the development of India’s aviation and industrial sectors. He served as the Chief Minister of Odisha twice and was known for his contributions to India's freedom struggle and international diplomacy.
